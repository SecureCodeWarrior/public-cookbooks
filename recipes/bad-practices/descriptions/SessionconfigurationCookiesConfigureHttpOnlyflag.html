Session cookies are frequently the target of Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) attacks. The default setting of the HttpOnly flag is false, which is insecure. By setting the setHttpOnly() to true, the cookie cannot be accessed by client-side scripts.
Before:Cookie cookie = new Cookie("name", "value");
After:Cookie cookie = new Cookie("name", "value"); cookie.setHttpOnly(true);
