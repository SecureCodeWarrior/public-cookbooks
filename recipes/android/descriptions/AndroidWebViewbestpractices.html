Out of Android best practices and secure coding guidelines, recommendations were abstracted for WebView and its configuration. Best practices for WebView settings are to turn off JavaScript to prevent XSS attacks, disallowing file access to prevent data leakages as well as turning off geolocation to avoid information leakage. Before: 
webView.getSettings().setJavaScriptEnabled(true);
webView.getSettings().setAllowFileAccess(true);
webView.getSettings().setGeolocationEnabled(true);
webView.getSettings().setAllowContentAccess(true);
After: 
webView.getSettings().setJavaScriptEnabled(false);
webView.getSettings().setAllowFileAccess(false);
webView.getSettings().setGeolocationEnabled(false);
webView.getSettings().setAllowContentAccess(false);
